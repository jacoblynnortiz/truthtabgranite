About Truth Tabernacle

Our Mission

Our Mission is to help people become what God has designed them to be, and as a result of such, live the abundant life in the blessings of God's Truth and Kingdom. God not only delivered Israel out of the bondage of Egypt, He also called them to their true identity where glorious blessings would be discovered. Truth Tabernacle is here to help people from every walk of life break free from the things that hold them back from those blessings, help them in the journey of formation, and ultimately help them secure the blessings of God and His Truth. At Truth Tabernacle you will experience the power of God's Spirit that can deliver any soul from the clutches of sin, lively worship and prayer from those who have been redeemed, and passionate Bible preaching and teaching to not only deliver souls from the grips that hold them back, but also to help them grow in the Truth. In a world full of division and growing contention, you will find a safe place where love binds humanity together before God. Most importantly, in a world full of confusion you will find clear answers with an ultimate end reward.

Our Story

The story of Truth Tabernacle is the story of redemption and resurrection. The church, under its former name, suffered and struggled for many years. However, by the grace of God, it has been redeemed, transformed, and resurrected into a new and glorious identity. The story of the church also reflects in the story of the members who have experienced the same redemptive and resurrection power. We invite you to pray about being the next chapter to this beautiful story.
