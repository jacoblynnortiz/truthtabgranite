Course Overview

Awestrucking it must have been when Jesus visited John on the Island of Patmos and gave him the ultimate revelation (apokalupsis) of all time. This course allows for the discovery of how the Book of Revelation is more than just an endtime book, although it does include the end of time. It will be discovered that John's writings are an ultimate Revelation of Jesus Christ, His Kingdom, and Church, and how even though great spiritual, physical, and ideological opposition will come, it will never defeat the eternal victory of Christ and the Church. This course focuses less on world events and rather more focuses on how the book reveals the fall of mankind, the Old Testament's purpose, the life and ministry of Jesus Christ, the outpouring of His Spirit, the establishing of His Kingdom, the escalating war between good and evil, and the climatic event of Christ's Return. Let's start our journey through the awestrucking Revelation Jesus gave to John on the Island of Patmos.

In "The Apokalupsis On Patmos" you'll learn:

  • A Book of Revelation
  • Daniel's Seventy Weeks & The Church Age
  • Twenty-Four Elders And Four Beasts
  • The Lion And The Lamb
  • Six Seals
  • The Seventh Seal
  • The Seventh Seal (Pt. 2)
  • Two Witness Prophets
  • The Woman And The Man Child
  • The Beast And False Prophet
  • The Redeemed
  • The Song Of Moses & Tabernacle Of Testimony
  • The Seven Vials
  • Babylon And The Beast
  • The Marriage Supper Of The Lamb
  • The Resurrection
  • New Heaven And New Earth
  • The Tree And River Of Life
