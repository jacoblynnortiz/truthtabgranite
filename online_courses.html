Let's Begin!

At Truth Tabernacle of Granite City, we are not ashamed of our lively worship, prayer, and praise. God has equipped humanity with a range of emotions, each serving a divine purpose in our pursuit of Him. Joy in the Lord is given for strength. Godly sorrow leads us to repentance. Gratefulness compels us to thanksgiving and praise. In our weeping, the Spirit is our Comforter. Yet, we do not build our faith on emotions alone.

The Church—the Bride of Christ—exists for intimate relationship with Jesus, the Bridegroom. True revelation is not merely knowledge; it is an unveiling, a divine intimacy between God and His people, leading us into covenant love. Without revelation, our worship is empty. Therefore, we embrace the guidance of the Holy Ghost, who leads us into all Truth, teaching us line upon line, precept upon precept.

In pursuit of this Truth, Truth Tabernacle has partnered with the Revealed Radio Network to provide Truth Academy—a resource for biblical and life learning. We invite you to join us in deepening your understanding of God, His Kingdom, and the world around us.
