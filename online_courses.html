Let's Begin!

While Truth Tabernacle is not ashamed of our passionate and affectionate praise and worship, we are not a church merely driven by emotion alone. It is believed that the affectionate expression of love is to help bring us closer to the Almighty God who wishes to also reveal Himself, bringing us to divine wisdom and understanding of Himself and the Truth. The word revelation by definition is the revealing through affection. It is an intimate word. Therefore, Truth Tabernacle believes in the balance of affectionate praise and Biblical revelation.

The following courses are to help impart doctrinal understanding of God, His plan, and how we fulfill His plan, both individually and collectively. We pray that as you embark on the journey through these courses your eyes of understanding will be enlightened and you will see the hope of your calling.
